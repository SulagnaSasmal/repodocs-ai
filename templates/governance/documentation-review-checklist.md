# Documentation Review Checklist

## Accuracy

- Does the document match the current system behavior?
- Are all examples aligned with real requests and responses?

## Completeness

- Are prerequisites documented?
- Are parameters, constraints, and outputs fully covered?

## Security

- Are authentication and authorization requirements explicit?
- Does the document avoid exposing secrets or unsafe examples?

## Clarity

- Is the language direct and unambiguous?
- Can the target reader complete the task without outside assumptions?

## Version Compatibility

- Is the relevant version identified?
- Are beta or deprecated states clearly marked?
