# Runbook: {runbook-name}

## Purpose

Describe the operational scenario this runbook covers and when it should be used.

## Preconditions

List required access, tooling, system state, or approvals.

## Triggers

Describe the alerts, symptoms, or manual requests that start this procedure.

## Step-by-Step Actions

1. Describe the first operational action.
2. Describe the second operational action.
3. Continue until the procedure is complete.

## Validation Checks

## Rollback Or Escalation

Describe rollback actions, safe stopping points, and escalation contacts.

## Audit Notes
